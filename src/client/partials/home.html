Welcome to (not) Yahtzee!

Rules

Each hand consists of three rolls of five dice.

You can hold dice between turns, just simply click on the one you wish to keep!

The goal is to earn as many points as possible! Each category has a different rule, simply mouse over the info circle to see how you can get points!


Accounts

With an account, you can keep track of your stats! You can keep track of:

Your high score

Average score

Yahtzees rolled

All time points scored

You can play without an account, it just wont keep track of your data!
